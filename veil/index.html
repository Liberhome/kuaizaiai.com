Veil Privacy Support

Veil — privacy shield for the AI-era Mac

One click hides every screen. Your Mac keeps running your AI agents, downloads, and renders. The moment your face returns, the shield lifts.

macOS 13 or later · Universal binary · No accounts, no network calls

Shield your screen on demand

Click the menu bar icon or press ⌃⌥⌘V. Every connected display blacks out. Your Mac keeps running.

Keep your Mac awake while shielded

Background tasks, downloads, and AI agents keep working. No more "the agent died because the screen slept."

Unlock with your face

Walk back to your desk and Veil sees you — the shield lifts automatically. Local on-device recognition only; your face never leaves your Mac.

Agent Mode

Set a 10 / 30 / 60-minute timer for an unattended AI session. Veil shows a live countdown so you know how long the task has been running when you return.

Auto-Shield (optional)

Let the camera watch your seat. The moment you stand up for 30 seconds, Veil pulls the shield. Walk-away protection without lifting a finger.

Break reminders — your prompts, your cadence

Tell Veil what to nudge you about. Set an interval (5–180 minutes). Veil quietly delivers one of your prompts to Notification Center — no popups, no screen interruptions.

Veil 屏息 · 为 AI 时代工位打造的屏幕卫士

电脑上跑着 Claude Code、Cursor 长任务、视频渲染——你还是得离座。倒杯咖啡、开个会、上个洗手间。一键屏息，电脑照常运行，路过的同事看不见屏幕。回来刷脸自动解锁。

一键屏息

点一下，或按全局快捷键 ⌃⌥⌘V，所有屏幕瞬间变黑。内容被遮住，电脑不睡。

屏息时保持运行

后台任务、下载、AI 代理继续跑。再也不会出现"agent 因屏幕睡眠而中断"。

刷脸自动解锁

你回到桌前，Veil 看见你，遮罩自动撤下。识别全程本地完成，人脸数据不离开你的 Mac。

Agent 模式

为无人值守的 AI 任务设个定时（10 / 30 / 60 分钟）。遮罩上显示倒计时，回来一眼知道跑了多久。

离座自动遮罩

摄像头守住座位。一旦离开 30 秒，Veil 自动盖上遮罩。不用动手的离座防护。

休息提醒（自定义节奏）

告诉 Veil 该提醒你什么——"起来活动颈椎"、"喝口水"。设一个间隔（5 到 180 分钟），通知中心安静地推送你写的提示。
