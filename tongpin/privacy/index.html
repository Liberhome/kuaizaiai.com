Privacy Policy

This policy describes the backend account paid-download version of 同频 Verve. The app uses Sign in with Apple for account functions and offers optional on-device Apple Health and YC smart-ring signals. Build 1.0 (16) has No in-app purchases and No subscriptions.

Current Version Boundary

The current version lets you manually choose today's status, sign in with Apple, manage account safety actions, and delete your account.

  • Sign in with Apple is used for account creation and session exchange.
  • Backend services store account, status, safety, and deletion records.
  • Apple may provide a relay email during Sign in with Apple; we store it only for account functionality.
  • No in-app purchases and No subscriptions are active in build 1.0.
  • No analytics, ads, tracking, or third-party SDK data collection.
  • Optional Apple Health read access is used only after you grant permission.
  • Optional Bluetooth scanning and connection are limited to YC SDK-verified smart rings that you choose.
  • No location access, contact access, camera access, microphone access, or photo-library access.

Data We Process

同频 Verve stores app-local preferences in iOS UserDefaults and processes backend data needed for account functionality.

  • Contact info, limited to the Apple relay email from Sign in with Apple when Apple provides it.
  • Identifiers, such as internal user ID, Sign in with Apple subject hash, and session token hash.
  • User content and safety records, such as manual daily status, profile/status fields, invitation messages and state, adult policy confirmation, block, report, mute, and remove-from-nearby actions.
  • On this device, a current Apple Health summary (sleep, resting heart rate, HRV, and steps), an optional YC ring name/MAC address/current heart rate, and manual private metrics. These values are used for your private status reference, retained locally, and never shown to other users.
  • Diagnostics only if needed to operate the service, prevent fraud, or investigate safety issues.

This data is not sold, not used for advertising, and not used to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Health And Wearable Signals

Apple Health is optional. When you grant access, the app may read sleep, resting heart rate, HRV, and step summaries to help you review today's private status. YC smart rings are also optional: the app scans only for devices verified by the YC SDK and can read the connected ring's current heart rate and basic device state. The app does not upload raw HealthKit samples, Bluetooth advertisements, or ring telemetry to the social surface, and it never uses these signals to rank people or decide consent. Xiaomi and other third-party bands are not currently connected.

Purchases

Build 1.0 is sold as a paid download through the App Store. It does not sell in-app purchases or subscriptions, and the app does not collect purchase history or subscription state.

Deletion

You can initiate account deletion in the app. We delete or anonymize account, profile, status, and session data where possible. We may retain limited safety, fraud-prevention, or legal records when required to prevent abuse or comply with law.

Future Changes

If a future version adds new wearable vendors, location, analytics, ads, tracking, raw health-data server sync, or additional data collection, this policy must be updated before that version is submitted. The App Store privacy answers must match the submitted binary exactly.
