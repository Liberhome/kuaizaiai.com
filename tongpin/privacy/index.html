Tongpin Privacy Policy

Effective: July 7, 2026 · Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the current local-only iOS version of Tongpin / 同频 (the "App"), provided by Kuaizai Intelligence (Chengmai) Technology Co., Ltd. ("we", "us", or the "Company"). Please read this policy before using the App.

1. Current Version Summary

The current version of Tongpin is a manual daily-status app. It does not require an account, does not connect to our servers, and does not collect personal data from you.

  • No sign-in or account creation.
  • No backend service or network sync.
  • No analytics, ads, tracking, or third-party SDK data collection.
  • No HealthKit or Apple Health reading.
  • No Bluetooth scanning, pairing, or wearable-device connection.
  • No location, contacts, camera, microphone, or photo-library access.

2. Data Stored On Your Device

Tongpin stores only the local manual status needed to show today's state in the App. This is stored in iOS app-local UserDefaults, together with the local date used to expire that status after the current day.

This data is not sent to us, not sold, not used for advertising, and not shared with other users.

3. Health And Wearable Signals

Some screens may mention Apple Watch, Apple Health, Xiaomi bands, other wearables, or smart rings as future optional signal sources. In the current submitted binary these are not active integrations. The App does not request HealthKit permission, does not read heart rate or sleep data, and does not scan for Bluetooth devices.

4. Apple's Role

If you obtain Tongpin through the App Store, Apple may process download, purchase, crash-reporting, and account information under Apple's own privacy practices. We do not receive your Apple ID, payment information, or App Store account credentials from Apple.

5. Deletion

Because the current version has no account and no server-side data, there is no remote account to delete. You can remove the local app data by deleting the App from your device. Today's manual status also expires automatically after the current day.

6. Future Changes

If a future version adds accounts, networking, HealthKit, Bluetooth devices, social features, or other data collection, this policy will be updated before that version is submitted. The App Store privacy answers must match the submitted binary exactly.

7. Contact

Email: liber@kuaizaiai.com

For any privacy-related question, deletion request, or complaint, please email us. We aim to respond within 15 business days.

同频隐私政策（中文）

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 7 日 · 最近更新：2026 年 7 月 7 日

本隐私政策说明同频 iOS 应用（以下简称"本应用"或"同频"）在当前本机版本中如何处理信息。本应用由快哉智能（澄迈）科技有限责任公司（以下简称"我们"或"本公司"）提供。请您在使用本应用前完整阅读本政策。

一、当前版本概述

当前版本的同频是一款手动设置每日状态的本机应用。不需要登录账号，不连接我们的服务器，也不向我们收集个人数据。

  • 没有登录或账号创建。
  • 没有后端服务或网络同步。
  • 没有分析、广告、追踪或第三方 SDK 数据收集。
  • 不会读取 HealthKit 或 Apple 健康。
  • 不会扫描、配对或连接蓝牙可穿戴设备。
  • 不会访问位置、通讯录、摄像头、麦克风或照片库。

二、本机存储的数据

同频只会在本机存储显示今日状态所需的手动选择。该数据保存在 iOS 应用本地 UserDefaults 中，并同时保存本地日期，用于在当天结束后自动失效。

这些数据不会发送给我们，不会出售，不会用于广告，也不会分享给其他用户。

三、健康与可穿戴信号

部分界面可能提到 Apple Watch、Apple 健康、小米手环、其他可穿戴设备或智能戒指，作为未来可选的私密参考来源。在当前提交版本中，这些集成尚未启用。本应用不会请求 HealthKit 权限，不会读取心率或睡眠数据，也不会扫描蓝牙设备。

四、Apple 的角色

如果您通过 App Store 获取同频，Apple 可能会依据其隐私实践处理下载、购买、崩溃报告和账号相关信息。我们不会从 Apple 获得您的 Apple ID、支付信息或 App Store 账号凭据。

五、删除

由于当前版本没有账号和服务器端数据，因此没有远程账号需要删除。您可以通过从设备上删除本应用来移除本机应用数据。今日手动状态也会在当天结束后自动失效。

六、未来变更

如果未来版本加入账号、联网、HealthKit、蓝牙设备、社交功能或其他数据收集，我们会在提交该版本前更新本政策。App Store 隐私答案必须与提交的二进制版本完全一致。

七、联系方式

邮箱：liber@kuaizaiai.com

如您有任何隐私相关疑问、删除请求或申诉，请通过上述邮箱与我们联系，我们将在 15 个工作日内回复。
