Privacy Policy

This policy describes the backend account and Verve membership version of 同频 Verve. The app uses Sign in with Apple, stores daily status and safety records with our backend, and uses StoreKit for Verve subscriptions.

Current Version Boundary

The current version lets you manually choose today's status, sign in with Apple, manage account safety actions, delete your account, and use Verve membership features.

  • Sign in with Apple is used for account creation and session exchange.
  • Backend services store account, status, safety, deletion, and membership entitlement records.
  • StoreKit is used for Verve monthly and yearly subscriptions.
  • No analytics, ads, tracking, or third-party SDK data collection.
  • No HealthKit or Apple Health reading.
  • No Bluetooth scanning, pairing, or wearable-device connection.
  • No location access, contact access, camera access, microphone access, or photo-library access.

Data We Process

同频 Verve stores app-local preferences in iOS UserDefaults and processes backend data needed for account functionality.

  • Identifiers, such as internal user ID, Sign in with Apple subject hash, and session token hash.
  • User content and safety records, such as manual daily status, profile/status fields, adult policy confirmation, block, report, mute, and remove-from-nearby actions.
  • Purchases, such as product ID, original transaction ID, subscription state, refund, revocation, billing retry, and grace-period state.
  • Diagnostics only if needed to operate the service, prevent fraud, or investigate safety issues.

This data is not sold, not used for advertising, and not used to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Health And Wearable Signals

Some screens may mention Apple Watch, Apple Health, Xiaomi bands, other wearables, or smart rings as future optional signal sources. In this version these are not active integrations. The app does not request HealthKit permission, does not read heart rate or sleep data, and does not scan for Bluetooth devices.

Verve Membership

Verve membership is handled through Apple's in-app purchase system. We use purchase and entitlement records only to unlock membership features, prevent fraud, handle refunds or revocations, and provide support.

Deletion

You can initiate account deletion in the app. We delete or anonymize account, profile, status, and session data where possible. We may retain limited safety, fraud-prevention, legal, or purchase records when required to operate subscriptions, process refunds, prevent abuse, or comply with law.

Future Changes

If a future version adds HealthKit, Bluetooth devices, location, analytics, ads, tracking, or additional data collection, this policy must be updated before that version is submitted. The App Store privacy answers must match the submitted binary exactly.
