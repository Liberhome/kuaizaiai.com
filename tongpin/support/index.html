What This Version Does

  • Lets you manually choose today's status on this device.
  • Uses Sign in with Apple for account access.
  • Supports account deletion, adult policy confirmation, and safety actions.
  • No in-app purchases and No subscriptions are active in build 1.0.
  • Stores app-local preferences and optional private signal summaries in iOS app-local UserDefaults.
  • Can read Apple Health summaries only after you grant HealthKit permission.
  • Can scan and connect only YC SDK-verified smart rings that you choose.

What This Version Does Not Do

  • No analytics, ads, tracking, or third-party profiling.
  • No Xiaomi or other third-party-band connection in this version.
  • No raw HealthKit samples, Bluetooth advertisements, or device telemetry are visible to other users.
  • No location, contacts, camera, microphone, or photo-library access.

Known Limits

Apple Health and YC rings are optional private sources. Nearby discovery requires sign-in, adult confirmation, and an explicit same-city opt-in; invitations are delivered only to the in-app inbox and can be accepted, declined, muted, blocked, reported, or cancelled.
