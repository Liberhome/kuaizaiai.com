Tongpin Support

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Contact

For support requests, bug reports, feature ideas, and privacy questions, please email liber@kuaizaiai.com. We aim to reply within 1 to 3 business days for product support and within 15 business days for privacy-related requests.

App Information

  • App name: Tongpin / 同频
  • Current version: 0.1
  • Current build: 1
  • Bundle ID: com.liberhome.tongpin
  • Current mode: local-only manual daily status.

What This Version Does

  • Lets you manually choose today's status on this device.
  • Stores that manual status locally in iOS app-local UserDefaults.
  • Expires the local manual status after the current day.

What This Version Does Not Do

  • No sign-in, account creation, or account deletion flow.
  • No internet connection, backend sync, analytics, ads, or tracking.
  • No HealthKit or Apple Health reading.
  • No Bluetooth scanning, pairing, or wearable-device connection.
  • No location, contacts, camera, microphone, or photo-library access.

Known Limits

Apple Watch, Apple Health, Xiaomi bands, other wearables, smart rings, accounts, nearby discovery, and invitations are not active in this local-only binary. They should only be enabled after the real implementation, safety flows, and matching App Store privacy answers are ready.

Troubleshooting

  • If today's status looks wrong, manually choose the status that best matches your day.
  • If a status does not persist, make sure the App was not deleted or reinstalled.
  • Today's manual status is designed to expire after the current day.

Reporting A Bug

Please email liber@kuaizaiai.com with:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • Tongpin version and build number.
  • Steps that led to the issue and what you expected to happen.
  • A screenshot or screen recording if it helps explain the issue.

Apple Watch、Apple 健康、小米手环、其他可穿戴设备、智能戒指、账号、附近发现和邀约功能，在当前本机版本中尚未启用。它们需要在真实实现、安全流程和 App Store 隐私答案准备完成后再开放。

常见排查

  • 如果今日状态不符合实际，请直接手动选择最符合今天的状态。
  • 如果状态没有保留，请确认应用没有被删除或重新安装。
  • 今日手动状态会在当天结束后自动失效，这是当前版本的设计。
